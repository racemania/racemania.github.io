Game Info  
  • ABOUT

    Racemania GP is the first edition of ultimate 3D racing game made just for mobile.

    It is not about who has the best car, its about who is the BEST RACER.

  • SOLO MODE

    In solo mode player is racing against the clock to try and beat current records. Player have to reach finish line as fast as possible.Your only mission: Pass the checkpoints! Your main challenge is the clock, and only fast and focused driving for each track will allow you to beat best times. There are 4 records to beat: BRONZE medal, SILVER medal, GOLD medal and finally AUTHOR TIME. It is always possible to return to the starting line or the checkpoint.

  • RACE ON 30 CHALLENGING TRACKS

    Race on 30 tracks organized in 3 Grand Prix - and that's just the starting line. Every track is carefully designed for fast and technical driving at the same time. Race time is less than 1 minute for each track! Win medals to unlock stages.

  • WATCH AND SHARE REPLAY WITH FRIENDS

    Watch and share race replays with friends.

  • MULTIPLE CONTROL SCHEMES

    7 available control schemes. And there is more: In-game adjust and customization for each scheme!

  • Over 90 Available Countries

    OVER 90 AVAILABLE COUNTRIES to choose. More coming soon. Car is painted using player country. We respect and love difference.

  • CUTTING-EDGE GRAPHICS.

    Beautiful graphics with modern 3D graphics engine , using hardware maximum capability.

  • STATE-OF-THE-ART CAR PHYSICS

    Real gem. Tuned for really easy drive, but you will really drive same track many times until master track. Jumps? Stunts? Drift? No problem.
